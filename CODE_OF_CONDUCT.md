# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming, safe, and inclusive environment for everyone participating in the SmartAid Platform project.  
We expect all contributors and community members to treat each other with respect and professionalism.

This project follows the principles of non-discrimination and “Do No Harm,” which align with Digital Public Goods (DPG) standards.

---

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

- Treat others with respect and empathy.
- Use inclusive and non-discriminatory language.
- Provide constructive feedback when reviewing contributions.
- Accept responsibility for their actions.
- Help create a positive environment for contributors of all backgrounds and skill levels.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.
- Insults, derogatory comments, or personal attacks.
- Sharing harmful, misleading, or offensive content.
- Any form of violence, threat, or intimidation.
- Publishing others’ private information without explicit permission.
- Actions that jeopardize the safety or integrity of the community.

---

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to:

- Warn contributors who violate the Code of Conduct
- Temporarily or permanently remove content that violates guidelines
- Ban users from participating in the project for repeated offenses
